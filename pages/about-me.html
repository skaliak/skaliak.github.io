Peter Lindberg

Software Engineer

I've been taking things apart to see how they worked since I was a small child. When I was 12, I ran a dial-up Bulletin Board Server off the family computer, much to my parents' frustration. I taught myself how to program about 8 years ago (Perl was my first language). For my job as a Systems Administrator, I wrote many small utilities and a few large applications, some of which were used by nearly everyone in the organization. Around the end of 2013, I realized I that programming was the most rewarding and enjoyable part of my job, so I enrolled in a Post-Bacc Computer Science degree program, quit my job, and became a full-time student. I finished my degree (in about 15 months) in March 2015, with a GPA of 3.9.
I am currently seeking full-time employment.
